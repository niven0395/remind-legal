# Privacy Policy for Remind

**Last Updated: January 2026**

Creation Methods Limited ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Remind mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

## 1. Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **Reminders and Notes**: Content you create within the app including reminder titles, descriptions, and notes
- **Calendar Data**: When you grant permission, we access your calendar events to provide smart suggestions
- **Location Data**: When you grant permission, we use your location for location-based reminders

### Automatically Collected Information
- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system version, and app version
- **Usage Data**: How you interact with the app to improve our services
- **Crash Reports**: Technical data to help us fix bugs and improve stability

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:
- Provide and maintain the Remind service
- Process your reminders and send notifications
- Sync your data across your devices via iCloud
- Provide AI-powered features and suggestions
- Process subscription payments through Apple
- Improve and optimize the app experience
- Send you important service updates

## 3. Data Storage and Sync

### iCloud Sync
Your reminders and notes are synced using Apple's iCloud service. This data is:
- Stored securely in your personal iCloud account
- Encrypted in transit and at rest by Apple
- Only accessible by you across your Apple devices

### Local Storage
Some data is stored locally on your device for offline functionality.

## 4. Third-Party Services

### Apple Services
- **iCloud**: For data synchronization
- **Apple Push Notification Service**: For reminder notifications
- **App Store**: For subscription management and payments

### AI Processing
- We use AI services to provide intelligent features
- Conversation data is processed securely and not used to train AI models
- We do not sell your personal data to third parties

## 5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information. We may share data only:
- With your consent
- To comply with legal obligations
- To protect our rights or the safety of users
- With service providers who assist in operating our app (under strict confidentiality)

## 6. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including:
- Encryption of data in transit (TLS/SSL)
- Secure authentication via Apple App Attest
- Regular security assessments

## 7. Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access**: Request a copy of your data
- **Delete**: Delete your account and associated data
- **Opt-out**: Disable optional features like location services
- **Export**: Export your data through iCloud
