# Remind Support

Welcome to Remind Support. We're here to help!

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Getting Started

**How do I create a reminder?**  
Tap the chat icon and simply tell Remind what you want to be reminded about. Our AI will understand natural language like "Remind me to call mom tomorrow at 3pm".

**How do I enable notifications?**  
When you first open the app, you'll be prompted to allow notifications. If you declined, go to Settings > Notifications > Remind and enable them.

**Does Remind sync across devices?**  
Yes! Remind uses iCloud to sync your reminders and notes across all your Apple devices signed into the same Apple ID.

### Subscription & Billing

**How do I manage my subscription?**  
1. Open the Settings app on your iPhone
2. Tap your name at the top
3. Tap "Subscriptions"
4. Select "Remind Pro" to manage or cancel

**How do I cancel my subscription?**  
Follow the steps above and tap "Cancel Subscription". You'll retain access until the end of your current billing period.

**How do I request a refund?**  
Refunds are handled by Apple. Visit [reportaproblem.apple.com](https://reportaproblem.apple.com) to request a refund.

**I was charged but don't have access**  
Try restoring your purchase: Open Remind > Paywall > Tap "Restore Purchases". If that doesn't work, contact us below.

### Troubleshooting

**Reminders aren't syncing**  
- Ensure iCloud is enabled: Settings > [Your Name] > iCloud > Remind
- Check your internet connection
- Try signing out and back into iCloud

**I'm not receiving notifications**  
- Check Settings > Notifications > Remind is enabled
- Ensure Focus/Do Not Disturb is not blocking notifications
- Verify the reminder has a valid date and time

**The app is crashing**  
- Ensure you're on the latest version from the App Store
- Try restarting your device
- If the issue persists, contact us with details

### Privacy & Data

**How do I delete my data?**  
Your data is stored in your personal iCloud account. To delete:
1. Delete reminders/notes within the app
2. Or delete the app entirely - your data will be removed from iCloud
